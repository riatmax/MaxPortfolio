// OVERVIEW.TXT

Feud is a 3D local multiplayer party game built in Unity where players compete to collect the most cherries by gathering from a shared tree or stealing from other players. I contributed core gameplay systems, including a modular event system, round management logic, and a flexible power-up system that supports dynamic gameplay interactions.
